How It Works

Match Simulation

  1. Build Your Team: Assemble 12 players with unique skills and positions
  2. Simulate Match: Our engine runs 100 rounds of realistic basketball action
  3. Fast Breaks: Steal the ball and trigger explosive scoring opportunities
  4. Strategic Shooting: Guards take 3s from mid-range, big men dominate the paint
  5. See Results: Realistic NBA-style scores (80-110 PPG) with bilingual narration

Probability System

Our D20 system ensures balanced, skill-based gameplay:

  • Shooting: d20 + skill + position modifier vs DC (12 for 2pt, 15 for 3pt)
  • Steals: 25% attempt rate within 5 squares, triggers fast breaks
  • Position Bonuses: Guards +3 for 3pt, Centers +3 for 2pt
  • Fast Break Bonus: +3 to shooting after successful steals
